TideBit-Defi

  • The product : TideBit-Defi is a crypto exchange platform that focus on decentralized financial trading.
  • Project duration : 1 year
  • Tool that I used

  • The Problem

    Cryptocurrency is a relatively new and unique form of investment that comes with a range of unfamiliar concepts and information. This can make it challenging for users to grasp, and as a result, many people find it overwhelming and give up on trying to use it.

  • The Goal

    To develop a user-friendly Crypto CFD De-Fi Trading platform with an intuitive interface and clear UX writing to assist users in easily adopting this new and innovative investment method.

  • My role

    UX Researcher
    UX Designer
    UI Designer
    Brand Designer

  • Responsibilities

    User research, Competitor audit, Wireframing, Mockup, Prototyping, CIS design.

user research

Understanding the user

Summary

I have recently conducted interviews with a diverse group of users, some of whom are already familiar with cryptocurrency while others are entirely new to the concept. Additionally, I administered an online survey that garnered participation from nearly 100 individuals. These interviews and survey responses have provided valuable insights and perspectives on the the product.

Pain Points

  • Users expressed that they find it challenging to dedicate significant effort to learn about cryptocurrencies and the associated terminology.

  • With other platforms, users found it difficult to use on their mobile devices due to complicated charts and a wordy interface.

  • The registration process for many cryptocurrency exchanges is often lengthy and cumbersome.

Starting the design

Wireframe

In this process, my objective is to develop the full functionality of our platform and address the pain points identified through previous research.

Mockup

In this stage, I have created design guidelines to align with the brand, emphasizing the importance of guiding users through the effective use of colors, varying font weights, icons, and more. The objective is to enable users to accomplish their goals on the website without relying on excessive text.
